About Us

Pink Onion events is a firm Located in Pakistan’s Capital that is meant to provide the best services for your event. We are a collection of strategists, planners and creators in a bid to offer a good event. Our innovativeness helps us engage clients and their audiences in a manner that reach all of their needs and requirements. As part of the marketing mix, events give businesses and individuals access to their target audience. We are therefore dedicated to making this a reality. Also, it is our aim to help our customers bring out the best in such interactions, hence creating a fulfilling and purposeful experience.

Events industry is coupled with a lot of challenges but at Pink Onion, we have strived to go beyond the challenges and offer the best to our clients. Our team is dedicated to serving you, our client in the most efficient and professional way. We also seek to offer successful events that are in line with your objectives, as well as offering advises on how we can better your function. Whether it’s a conference, wedding ceremony, an award’s dinner, team building or a graduation party, we deliver quality services at the best prices that resonate with your goals.

Besides from events, Pink Onion is a firm has for a long time now been conscious of giving back to the society in the best way possible. Charity has therefore been a key activity we are actively involved in. With the many poor families existing in our nation, victims of war as well as the sick, we have gone a long way in giving a helping hand as part of our CSR goal. Every year, we visit more than ten children homes, provide food, clothing and spend time with the children. Our future goal is to come up with a children’s home that will cater for more than 10,000 children from street families and poor backgrounds as well.

Our aim is to be the best events and charity firm in Pakistan, providing the best services and offering a charitable hand to the poor population in the country. To experience more of these, visit our contact address page to experience more of this. We are dedicated to make our clients happy as well as promoting the virtue of charity and kindness all over the country. With you and I, we can make this be a reality.
