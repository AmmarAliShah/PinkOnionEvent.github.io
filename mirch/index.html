About Us

P ink Onion brings you Mirch Masala. Here we offer you the best exquisite dishes in town. From Desi Masala Rice to Mirchi Desi, Fried & street style and Karahi foods. We combine a wide range of world cuisines with savory Pakistani food. Particular highlights of our specialties include original Chicken Biryani, Chicken Pulao, Afghani Pulao, Mutton Karahi, Chicken Shahlik, and Bhindi Gohst amongst many other delicious dishes. Our authenticity extends all the way to the interiors of our restaurants, the cool atmosphere which allows our guests enjoy their Pakistani cuisines in a lush environment.
Further, Mirch Masala offers a combination of cultural and the culinary, specializing in the best Pakistani dishes and other world best dishes. Our themed restaurant experience provides an outdoor dining in unique surroundings which bring about a feel of traditions blended with modernity. Buffet service is our priority where we give our clients the opportunity to have freedom and enjoy the dishes they like most. We are focused on introducing new dishes on a regular basis to keep up with the growing tastes and preferences of our customers. Understanding this helps us to maintain clients and offer the best services in a personalized manner.
Our location is perfectly situated in the capital which bring out a major appeal of the restaurant. It comes more alive at night with the exclusive lighting reflecting on the pools and the surroundings. We have excelled at offering first class services. We boast in gaining a reputation in whole of the country as the best restaurant in the region. Visit us today and receive the best culinary services at affordable prices suited just for you. We are dedicated to providing the best experience for our clients giving them an opportunity to receive the best Pakistani experience in all of the South Asia.
